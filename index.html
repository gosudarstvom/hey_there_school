About Us

关于我们

Hi!

We are Speaxie, a young and passionate team of professional English teachers!

We both lived and worked in China for more than 6 years. Since we gained most of our teaching experience in China, we know a lot about the peculiarities of working with Chinese kids of all ages.

We utilized our experience to develop an outstanding program with a unique approach to every student to help them master their English skills through fun and exciting interactive experiences.

Looking forward to bringing your English to a whole new level!

Hi!

我们是话仙子，年轻而充满激情的专业英语教师团队

我们都在中国生活和工作了6年多了。由于我们在中国获得了大量的教学经验，我们更加了解中国孩子们在各个年龄阶段的学习特点。

基于这些经验，我们专门为每一位中国学生量身定制开发了独特的课程，帮助他们通过深入、有趣和令人兴奋的互动体验掌握英语技能。我们保证你在我们的课堂上永远不会感到无聊，并且我们保证你在短短的几节课后就会注意到你的口语和听力有所提高。

期待您的英语水平再上一个新台阶！

Teacher Julie

Hi, everyone!

My name is Julie and I'm a professional English teacher. I specialize in teaching preschoolers and primary school students. Teaching English to kids can often be very challenging. That's why I use individual approach that will help children to learn English in a fun and playful way. My goal is to encourage students to freely communicate in English without fear of making mistakes.

大家好！

我叫Julie。我是一位专业英语教师。我专门负责学龄前儿童和小学生的英语教学。教孩子们英语常常是很有挑战性的，这就是为什么我使用一些特殊的教学方法，这将有助于孩子们以一种有趣的好玩的方式学习英语。我的目标是鼓励学生用英语自由交流，而不必担心犯错。
